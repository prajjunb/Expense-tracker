üìñ Frequently Asked Questions
How do I get started with FinFlow? ‚ñº
Start by creating an account and adding your first expense. Go to the Dashboard to see your spending overview, then navigate to Reports for detailed insights. Check Settings to customize your theme and currency preferences.
Where is my data stored? ‚ñº
All your expense data is stored locally on your device using browser storage (localStorage). This means your data is private and not sent to any server. For safety, regularly export your data as backup.
How do I export my expenses? ‚ñº
Visit the Reports page and use the export buttons: CSV (for Excel), JSON (for backup), HTML (for web), or Print/PDF. Choose the format that works best for your needs.
Can I change the currency symbol? ‚ñº
Yes! Go to Settings and update your currency symbol. All amounts across the app will automatically update to use your chosen currency.
How do I backup my data? ‚ñº
Export your expenses as JSON from the Reports page. This creates a complete backup file. Store it safely, and you can import it later if needed.
What happens if I clear my browser cache? ‚ñº
Clearing your browser cache will delete all stored data. To prevent data loss, export your expenses regularly to a safe location.
Is there a mobile app? ‚ñº
FinFlow is a web app that works on all devices through your browser. You can bookmark it on your phone or desktop for quick access. No separate app installation needed.
How do I contact support? ‚ñº
You can reach our team through the WhatsApp community or Instagram. Connect with us directly for quick support and updates!
